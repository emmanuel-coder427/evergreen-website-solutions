Many offices still rely heavily on paper. However, making small changes can significantly reduce paper waste.

Simple Tips to Reduce paper waste in Offices:

  1. Use digital documents instead of printing.
  2. Print double-sided whenever possible.
  3. Encourage employees to use reusable notebooks.
  4. Recycle uses paper into notepads
