Recycling office paper waste reduces the need for new paper production, saving trees, energy and water. by recycling paper, we prevent large amounts of waste from ending up in landfills.

Did you know? Recycling just one ton of paper saves;

  • 17 trees.
  • 7,000 gallons of water
  • Enough Electricity to power a home for six months.

As an office manager or employee, you can play a crucial role in reducing paperr waste by setting up recycling programs and encouraging digital communication
